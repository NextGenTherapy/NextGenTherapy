## Part F: Q&A (Pre-populate)

Add these questions to GBP Q&A section with owner answers:

### Q1: What type of therapy do you offer?
**A:** I'm a psychodynamic psychotherapist. That means we work together to understand why certain patterns keep showing up in your life — not just manage symptoms, but understand where they come from. Sessions are weekly and open-ended, as long as the work is useful.

### Q2: Do you offer online therapy?
**A:** Yes, I offer online therapy across the UK for anyone aged 16 or older. Sessions are via secure video call. I don't offer online therapy for under-16s as confidentiality is hard to guarantee when clients are at home with family present.

### Q3: How much does therapy cost?
**A:** Sessions are £60 for 50 minutes, paid weekly. The free 15-minute consultation call has no cost and no commitment — it's just a conversation to see if we're a good fit.

### Q4: Do you speak Romanian?
**A:** Yes! I offer therapy in both English and Romanian. You can work entirely in Romanian, switch between languages mid-session, or stick with English — whatever feels right for you.

### Q5: What ages do you work with?
**A:** I work with children from age 4, teenagers (13-17), young adults, and adults up to 40. Children and teenagers under 16 need to come to sessions in person at my Colchester practice. Online therapy is available for ages 16+.

## Part G: Reviews Strategy

### BACP Ethical Guidelines

- Do not solicit reviews directly
- Do not offer incentives for reviews
- Can mention that reviews help others find therapy
- Respond professionally to all reviews

### Review Response Template (Positive)

```
Thank you for taking the time to share your experience. I'm glad the work has been helpful.
```

### Review Response Template (Concerns)

```
Thank you for your feedback. If you'd like to discuss this further, please contact me directly so we can address your concerns.
```

### Building Reviews Ethically

- Add "Leave a review" link to post-therapy email (optional, not required)
- Natural mentions: "If you've found therapy helpful, a Google review helps others find support"
- Focus on excellent service — reviews follow naturally
