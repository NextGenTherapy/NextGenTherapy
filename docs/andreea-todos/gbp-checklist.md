## Review Handling

### BACP Ethical Constraints
- **Cannot** solicit reviews from current clients
- **Cannot** offer incentives for reviews
- **Can** respond to reviews professionally
- **Can** ask former clients (after therapy ended) if they'd like to share feedback

### Response Templates

**Positive review response:**
> Thank you for sharing your experience. I'm glad the work we did together was helpful.

**Negative review response:**
> I'm sorry to hear about your experience. Client confidentiality prevents me from discussing specifics publicly. If you'd like to discuss this further, please contact me directly or raise a concern with BACP.

- [ ] Monitor reviews weekly
- [ ] Respond to all reviews within 48 hours
